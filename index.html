Privacy Policy for KursBot

Effective Date: July 12, 2025

1. What Data We Collect

We only collect public Telegram user data such as your username and chat ID required for the bot's functionality. No sensitive personal data is collected.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is only used to:

  • Respond to commands like exchange rate lookup
  • Perform currency conversions

We do not share or sell your data to third parties.

3. Data Storage

The bot does not store your messages permanently. Exchange rates are fetched from third-party sources and are not tied to your identity.

4. Contact

For any concerns or questions, contact us at support@digilunar.com.

If you do not specify a Privacy Policy, the Telegram Standard Privacy Policy will apply.
